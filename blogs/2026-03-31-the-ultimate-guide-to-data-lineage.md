Data lineage tracks where data originates, how it transforms through systems, and where it surfaces for business use, covering both table-level and column-level lineage. The guide distinguishes technical lineage (for engineers debugging pipelines) from business lineage (for compliance and executives), and argues lineage only delivers value when automated, field-level specific, contextually rich, and searchable rather than just visually displayed.
